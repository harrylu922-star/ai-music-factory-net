About us

The name AI Music Factory comes from three ideas: AI (artificial intelligence), Music, and Factory. We are building an AI-driven music factory—helping creators complete composition, arrangement, and production efficiently and at scale, so the path from idea to finished track is shorter and more controllable.

We serve independent musicians, content creators, and teams that need volume music output with AI-powered composition, arrangement, and vocal and instrument generation, plus pipeline-style workflows and templates to increase output without sacrificing quality.

Features

  • AI composition and arrangement: melody, harmony, and arrangement generation in multiple styles and moods.
  • Pipeline-style production: template-based, reusable workflows for scalable and custom music production.
  • Vocal and instrument generation: AI-generated vocals and instruments to expand creative and demo options.
  • For creators and teams: a single, efficient environment for solo creators and teams with high-volume needs.
